Our Story

From bedside to codebase

Nexus Clinical started with a simple question — why are the tools clinicians rely on every day so far behind the care they deliver? Built by an RN and a small development team, Nexus combines clinical expertise with thoughtful design. Every feature is shaped by real workflows, real needs, and the understanding that at the bedside, every second matters.

We're not a big corporation. We're a small team that still works shifts and still codes from the bedside. That proximity to patient care isn't just our origin story — it's how we build every single day.
