Bridging the Gap Between
Knowledge and Practice

Nexus exists to solve a critical problem in healthcare: the fragmentation between clinical education and bedside reality. Nurses juggle separate apps for studying, shift management, communication, and decision-support—context-switching costs time, energy, and potentially patient safety. We believe technology should work FOR nurses, not add to their burden. Every nurse deserves an intelligent co-pilot that connects their knowledge base, their daily workflow, and their team in real-time. That's Nexus—one neural operating system where everything is interconnected, intuitive, and designed around how you actually work.

Our Story

Nexus was born from frustration. In 2022, our founding team—a group of RNs, NPs, and CNOs from top health systems—realized no single platform existed that bridged clinical education, shift management, and real-time decision support. We were switching between 5+ apps daily, losing critical context.

After 18 months of development with input from 200+ nurses across ICU, emergency, med/surg, and specialty units, Nexus launched in beta. Within 6 months, we had 2,000 active users and a 4.8/5 rating. Today, Nexus is used by nursing teams in 50+ hospitals and 30+ nursing schools.

We're not done. Our roadmap includes real-time telemetry integration, AI-powered shift handoffs, and partnerships with EHR providers. But our north star remains unchanged: technology that amplifies nursing expertise and protects patient safety.
