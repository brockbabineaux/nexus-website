Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel anytime?
▼
Yes, absolutely. You can cancel your subscription at any time, no questions asked. Your access will continue until the end of your billing period. No hidden fees or cancellation charges.
Is there a free trial?
▼
Yes! We offer a 7-day free trial with full access to all Nexus Pro features. No credit card required to start your trial. After the trial ends, your subscription is $9.99/month.
Do you offer student and veteran discounts?
▼
Yes! Veterans and students get 20% off with ID.me verification, bringing your rate down to just $7.99/month. Verify your status during checkout and the discount will apply immediately.
Is my payment information secure?
▼
Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption (SSL/TLS) and never store full credit card information on our servers. All payments are processed through secure, PCI-compliant payment gateways. Your financial data is protected.
Can I switch plans?
▼
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan anytime. If you upgrade, you'll be charged a prorated amount for the remainder of your current billing period. If you downgrade, your next billing will reflect the lower price.
Do you offer team or enterprise pricing?
▼
Yes! Hospitals, nursing schools, and healthcare organizations can get custom team pricing and dedicated support. Contact our sales team at support@nxsclinical.com or reach out through our contact form to discuss your organization's needs.
