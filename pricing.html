Questions?

Is Nexus really free?
Yes, Nexus is completely free during our beta period. After we launch out of beta, the Complete Access plan will be $9.99/month. No hidden fees, no surprises.
Who is Nexus for?
Nexus is designed for licensed healthcare professionals and nursing students who need fast, reliable access to clinical information. Whether you're a physician, nurse practitioner, PA, or nursing student, Nexus gives you the tools to make better clinical decisions.
Is my data safe?
Your privacy is our top priority. Nexus uses local storage, which means your data stays on your device. We're built with a HIPAA-conscious architecture, and no protected health information (PHI) ever leaves your device. You're always in control.
Can I use Nexus offline?
Absolutely. Core features like medication lookup, calculators, and flashcards work entirely offline. This means you can access critical clinical information anywhere, anytime—even without an internet connection.
How do I cancel?
You can cancel anytime with no penalties, no contracts, and no questions asked. We're confident you'll love Nexus, but if you ever want to cancel, it's just a few clicks.
